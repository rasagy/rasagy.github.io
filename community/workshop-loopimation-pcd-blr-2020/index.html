Loopimation was a short workshop on building looping geometric animations in P5.js that I conducted at Processing Community Day 2020 in Bengaluru (India) .

You can explore some of the example starter P5.js sketches , using the wonderful p5.createLoop library .

Slides are available on Google Slides :

You can also download & print my storyboarding template for planning your animations. More details on the Github repository for the workshop.
