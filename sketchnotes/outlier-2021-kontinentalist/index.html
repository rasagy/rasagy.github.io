Mick Yang & Isabella Chua spoke at Outlier about the amazing work by Kontinentalist team.

This was my favorite talk from Day #1. They showcased a series of inspiring & unique data stories based on their cultural experiences. From Rohingya refugees to chillis & queues in Asia — so many interesting topics!

What I loved was their process of creating these beautiful data stories:

  • Write for yourself
  • Find an angle + be proudly niche!
  • Unpack diversity
  • Test conventional wisdom
  • Find quantifiable data
